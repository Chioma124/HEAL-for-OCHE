About Us

Bridging medicine and education at Stanford

The Office of Child Health Equity (OCHE) at Stanford Medicine's Department of Pediatrics is committed to ensuring that every child — regardless of medical complexity, language, or background — can access the educational support they need and deserve.

Our Resource Repository is a centralized hub built for families, clinicians, educators, and discharge planners navigating the intersection of pediatric health and school services.
